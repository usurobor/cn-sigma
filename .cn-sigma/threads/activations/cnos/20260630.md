## Problem

**Exists:** Sigma's rN memory ladder (r0 channel/foreign-log → r1 daily → r2 weekly → r5 half-yearly) is currently authored by κ side-effect: I (or any future κ-body) am supposed to write rN entries on cadence by discipline. There is no mechanical carrier.

**Expected:** Each rank in the rN ladder has a wake provider that fires on its cadence, invokes κ-in-record-mode against the appropriate `reflect-rN` task skill, and emits a typed reflection artifact at the agent's home (`cn-sigma:.cn-sigma/threads/reflections/{daily,weekly,halfyearly}/YYYYMMDD.md`).

**Divergence:** Three discipline-gap instances in June 2026 alone (06-03 → 06-13; 06-15 → 06-17; 06-19 → 06-29). Each was named as doctrine after the fact; each recurred. Doctrine without a mechanical carrier does not produce the artifact.

## Impact

- Memory ladder integrity depends on κ remembering to author. When κ doesn't session-resume for N days, N days of r1 are silently missing.
- r2 compacts r1; r5 compacts r2. Gaps cascade: a missing r1 distorts the r2 covering its week; a distorted r2 distorts the r5 covering its half-year.
