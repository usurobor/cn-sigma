## Why the integration matters

The v0 essay made the model/system axis argument. v0.8.0 grounds it: if "receipts over vibes" is the closing tagline, *what counts as a receipt* needs a typed coherence block. TSC supplies that grammar. The essay can now stand on TSC rather than gesturing at it.
